# Tesla Social Media Analytics Using Excel

## 📌 Description

This project analyzes **Tesla's social media performance across multiple platforms** using data analytics techniques. The analysis focuses on engagement metrics, hashtag performance, campaign effectiveness, and follower growth trends to understand how Tesla’s social media strategy performs.

The project includes **data preprocessing, engagement analysis, platform comparison, campaign evaluation, and strategic insights**.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Clean and preprocess social media dataset
* Analyze engagement across platforms
* Identify top-performing hashtags and content types
* Evaluate campaign effectiveness and ROI
* Study follower growth and retention trends
* Provide strategic recommendations for social media campaigns

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Data Cleaning Techniques
* Data Visualization (Charts & Graphs)

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

### Data Preprocessing

* Removed duplicate entries
* Standardized date and platform formats
* Converted engagement metrics into numeric format
* Separated and cleaned hashtags

### Engagement Analysis

* Calculated engagement rate using:

  Engagement Rate = (Likes + Shares + Comments) / Impressions

* Identified top 10 Tesla posts with highest engagement

* Compared engagement across platforms and content types

### Platform Performance Analysis

* Compared engagement rates across platforms
* Measured follower growth rates
* Analyzed engagement vs advertising spend

### Hashtag and Content Strategy

* Identified most frequently used Tesla hashtags
* Compared performance of posts by hashtag
* Analyzed best content types per platform

### Campaign Effectiveness

* Calculated campaign engagement metrics
* Measured engagement uplift during campaigns
* Evaluated campaign ROI based on engagement vs budget

### Follower Growth Analysis

* Calculated weekly follower growth
* Identified peak follower gain periods
* Used moving averages to analyze trends
* Examined correlation between ad spend and follower growth

## 📈 Key Insights

* **Highest engagement platform:** Instagram
* **Highest follower growth platform:** YouTube
* **Best performing hashtag:** #TeslaCoInnovation
* **Highest ROI campaign:** CybertruckLaunch
* **Strongest follower growth campaign:** SustainabilityDrive

## 📢 Strategic Recommendation

A **multi-platform strategy** is recommended:

* Focus on **Twitter and YouTube** for campaign growth
* Maintain presence on other platforms for broader audience reach
